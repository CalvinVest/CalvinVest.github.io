Welcome to my website

Welcome to my website!

My name is Calvin Vest, and I'm a computer science student. This is my website, that I put together largely because I wanted a way to collect and display my projects across my different hobbies and skills. I'm a hobby genealogist and programmer, and I've transferred universities a couple times. All of this information and more is available on my About page.

For now, take a look around! I've always wanted a central convenient way to show off my hobbies and accomplishments, and I think I've done pretty well in organizing things. Take a look at the next section if you'd like some suggestions on where to go from here.

If you've got some suggestions or concerns about the site or the content in it, please reach out to me. My contact information can be found here.
