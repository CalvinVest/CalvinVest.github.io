The Surname Index

Please read this introduction!

This page is an alphabetical index of the profiles in the family trees I've constructed for my own genealogy, Hannah's, and Matt's.

The three indexes are in their own sections so you can minimize the ones you're not interested in. Surnames for each tree are sorted alphabetically. The lineage maps to the right of each surname do not indicate that number of generations, they simply allow you to have an understanding of how each branch connects with another. More detailed information on ancestry and vital information can be found on the individual profile pages linked within each ancestor's place in their index below.

A star indicates that the entry is a direct ancestor of the subject of that tree. (Calvin, Hannah, or Matt.)
