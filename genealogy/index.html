Genealogy Homepage

Genealogy has been a hobby of mine for over a decade.

I remember when I had a homework assignment, maybe fourth grade, to make a simple family tree of my parents and grandparents. It wasn't anything sophisticated, but by the fifth grade I'd started assembling my own family tree and extending branches farther and farther back. I developed a love for genealogy as a hobby, and began to spend more and more time on my own family tree, and soon began to build trees for my friends and help them discover their own family histories. That's part of why I built this part of the website: I want a way to share my work with the people that matter most to me.

For friends and family

Some of you are one here because you want to see the work I've done to preserve our family's history and this website is a way for me to showcase to you the most noteworthy of what I've discovered, and an easy way to navigate to the rest of what my hard work has to offer.

You might also be here because I've dedicated time to discovering what I can of your family tree. At the time I'm writing this, there's only one friend I've put work on this website for. So Hannah, if you're reading this, I've put a link directly on the sidebar to the left for you to head to a homepage I've made specifically for your family genealogy on this site. Everything else will be accessible from there. 🙂
