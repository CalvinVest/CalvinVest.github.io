I'm a Computer Science major.

I've been a student at Western Governor's University since 2021, earning my BSCS.

I've moved schools a couple times.

I first started college courses in the fall of 2014, as a dual-credit high school student. I started as an Engineering major. Since then, I've transferred to Southern Illinois University as an Electrical Engineering, than an Electrical and Computer Engineering student. Around the time of the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, I chose to change direction, and start pursuing a degree in computer science. I changed my major and transferred to Western Governor's University, and I've been there since.
