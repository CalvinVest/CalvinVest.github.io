About Me

Who am I?

I'm just a guy, closing on 30, with a bunch of hobbies and projects that I keep myself busy with in my free time.

I'm a computer science student, I work in the meat department of a grocery store, I'm an amateur genealogist, a huge Star Wars fan, and I play too many video games.

What is this?

This is my website! I wanted a place to show everything off. My hobbies, projects I've worked on, things I just think are cool, ways to connect with professional contacts and gaming buddies alike, and a chance for me to keep my web development skills fresh.

Granted, this website isn't anything special. Just some bare-bones HTML with some styling and scripting to make it look pretty, but the content and functionality itself are all pretty low-grade. I've had plans for how to improve this place ever since its inception, but I really only work on this in bursts (this is getting added May '25, for what it's worth.)
